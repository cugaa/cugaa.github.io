One question we get asked a lot is how your puppy will cope when being dropped off at the salon what you can do to help them? The best way to deal with this I have found is just for you to be confident for them and have a no fuss attitude when you leave them, then they know from you (mum or dad) that everything is going to be ok and there is nothing for them to worry about. Once you have left there is so much going on that they are happy and curious to see everything thats going on and we will make it a fun time for them!
